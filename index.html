How to Play

Getting started with Monster Survivor is simple. Use your keyboard or mouse to control your character's movement and aim your attacks. Collect experience orbs from defeated monsters to level up and choose powerful upgrades. The key to survival is finding the right combination of abilities and positioning yourself strategically to avoid being overwhelmed.

As you progress, you'll encounter different types of monsters, each with unique behaviors and attack patterns. Learn their movements, adapt your strategy, and see how long you can survive against the endless onslaught!

Tips for Success

Master the art of survival with these essential tips: Always keep moving to avoid being surrounded, prioritize area-of-effect upgrades for crowd control, collect experience orbs efficiently by planning your movement path, and save powerful abilities for critical moments when you're overwhelmed.

Remember that each run is an opportunity to learn and improve. Experiment with different upgrade combinations, discover synergies between abilities, and develop your own unique survival strategies to achieve higher scores and longer survival times.
